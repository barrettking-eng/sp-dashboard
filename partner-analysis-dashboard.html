⚠ Data Quality Notes & Assumptions
  • Domain matching method: A billing account is classified as "partner-owned" if the partner's email domain (e.g., agency.com) matches the billing account's billing_email domain. Gmail/Yahoo/generic addresses may cause misclassification — treat as approximate.
  • Match rate gap: 57.4% of PartnerStack partners (3,274) have no matching Close user account. These may use different emails, haven't signed up, or are inactive.
  • Client cost attribution: The ~$74K attributed MRR uses a proportional seat model applied to billable-only embedded partner users (616). A marginal-cost model yields ~$137K. Prior figures (~$112K / ~$247K) included all 720 embedded partners regardless of is_billable status.
  • Billable status source: user_org_memberships.is_billable is the authoritative per-user billing flag. 120 partners (9%) had no membership record and are shown as "unknown" — these may be inactive or on a different org.
  • billing_accounts.monthly_billable_amount is a current snapshot, not historical. MRR figures reflect the account's state as of the latest Snowflake sync.
  • Amplitude data was not available for this analysis. Activity and engagement validation of partner users could not be performed.
  • March 2026 partner join data is partial (20 of 31 days) — the 100 new partners this month understates the full-month pace.
